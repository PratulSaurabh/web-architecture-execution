# 1.1.10: Proxies, CDNs, and Load Balancers

- **Forward Proxy:** Sits in front of clients to filter or anonymize their outgoing traffic.
- **Reverse Proxy:** Sits in front of servers to protect and manage incoming traffic.
- **Load Balancer:** A type of reverse proxy that distributes traffic across multiple backend servers to improve scalability and reliability.
- **CDN (Content Delivery Network):** A geographically distributed network of proxy servers that caches content close to users, dramatically improving performance for a global audience by reducing latency.
